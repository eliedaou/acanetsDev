Research

While his research interest spans broadly in computer architecture and network systems, Prof. Yan Luo's current research focus on heterogeneous architecture and systems, software defined networking and deep learning. Dr. Luo directs the Laboratory of Advanced Computing and Networking Systems at UMass Lowell.

Teaching

Dr. Luo teaches courses on microprocessors, embedded systems, and advanced computer architecture. Hands-on practices are essential in them in addition to lectures in classrooms. Course projects may lead to thesis or dissertation research. No matter where you live, we have course materials and recorded tutorials that you can access from anywhere.
